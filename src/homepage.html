    My Journey

    High School

    I started my journey with coding in high school, where I was lucky to have a teacher who saw potential in my curiosity and enthusiasm. Their encouragement led me to delve deeper into computer science. Coding challenges became puzzles that I was eager to solve, and I found joy in creating functional, efficient solutions. My teacher's inspiring words always echoed in my mind, motivating me to never stop learning and experimenting.

    University Applications

    "As I neared the end of my high school years, I was faced with the choice of my future path. I applied to study Computer Engineering in University, driven by the dream of becoming a software engineer. It was a field that promised endless opportunities for learning and innovation, two aspects that I cherished deeply."

    The Current Stage

    However, life took an exciting turn, and I found myself accepting an apprenticeship at a prestigious company called Leonardo. Opting for an apprenticeship over traditional university education allowed me to immerse myself in the practical aspects of the field. It offered me an invaluable opportunity to learn from seasoned professionals and gain hands-on experience. This blend of learning and doing has been instrumental in shaping me into the engineer I am today.
